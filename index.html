I'm currently a third year student at Penn State University based in University Park, PA, pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Digital Arts and Media Design.

There has always been a part of me that yearns for artistic expression. When I entered college, I only had plans to pursue a degree in Computer Science. However, after discovering the Digital Arts and Media Design major, I knew it would be the perfect complement to both diversify my opportunities and to help me pursue my personal interests in indie game and web development.

I consider myself a software developer first and foremost. I began coding in high school when a teacher I was close to introduced me to HTML and CSS. But, if I'm going to be honest, I wasn't very interested. It wasn't until I took an introductory Python course the following year that I truly became hooked.

Nowadays, I have experience in scientific computing with Python, systems programming in C and Rust, front-end development in JavaScript and frameworks like React, and am interested in everything in-between. I'm passionate about open-source and hope to some day contribute to some of my favorite projects, such as Godot or Bevy.

Digital art and design has always been an interest of mine. In fact, graphic design was my dream profession as a child, and was a pretty serious hobby of mine for a few years. I have particular interest in UI/UX design as well as 3D modeling, rendering, and computer graphics. My biggest dream is to be a successful indie game developer, and I enjoy pursuing this as a hobby.

Despite my passion for Computer Science, my education felt like it was missing something before my double major. Digital design is integral to software development; software is, in the majority of cases, written to be interacted with and understood by humans, and design is very much the philosophy of how humans interact and understand.

In my free time, I love to engage with music. Throughout my childhood, I studied piano, with an interest in classical music. I have experimented in music production, and enjoy messing around with my keyboard and listening to different sounds.

Someday I would also like to learn to play the guitar that I bought.
